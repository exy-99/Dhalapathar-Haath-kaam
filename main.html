Our Story

At Dhalapathar Haath Kaam, we are passionate about preserving the rich heritage of handloom weaving. Our journey began in the quaint village of Dhalapathar, where skilled artisans have been crafting exquisite textiles for generations. Each piece we create is a labor of love, reflecting the intricate artistry and cultural significance of our craft. We believe in sustainable practices and empowering local communities, ensuring that every purchase supports the livelihoods of our weavers. Join us in celebrating tradition and craftsmanship with every thread woven into our collections.
