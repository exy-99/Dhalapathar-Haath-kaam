Our Story

Dhalapathar Pardas: Unique Yet Endangered

The Dhalapathar Parda, a hand-woven curtain or screen used for windows and doors, has been traditionally woven at Dhalapathar and Kalapathar villages, in Khurda district, Odisha. The Dhalapathar Pardas are woven on hand-looms with thick hand-spun cotton yarns. They are woven by women and men weavers from the Rangani community.
